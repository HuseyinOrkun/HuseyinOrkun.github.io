Hi! I am Hüseyin, I graduated from computer science in 2020 from Bilkent University and now I am completing my M.S. degree in Neuroscience at Bilkent University. Currently, I am a member of <a href='http://web3.bilkent.edu.tr/ccn/'>Computational Cognitive Neuroscience Lab (Urgen Lab)</a> headed by Burcu Aysen Urgen.

I am interested in neuroscience, programming and datasicence. During my M.S. degree I have worked on topics such as predictive processing, decision making, biological motion perception and action observation with behavioral and neuroimaging experiments. I have a computational background and I like to use my computational skills and knowledge of computer sciece to extract information from behavioral and neuroimaging data. You can see more of what I do in my <a href='/cv'> cv </a>.

Finally, I am passionate about open science and software, and I try to contribute to development of open source packages when I can. This website is where I am starting to place and write about my projects and my career.
